About Scholar SA

Scholar SA was founded in 2022 by a passionate group of individuals who wanted to make a difference in the lives of children. Our mission is to support children in school with uniforms, school shoes, extra classes, sports programs, and feminine hygiene products.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower children by providing them with the resources they need to succeed academically, explore their talents, and build a brighter future.

Our Vision

Our vision is a world where every child, regardless of their background, has access to education, sports, and personal development opportunities.

Our Impact

Since our founding, we have supported over 500 children with school uniforms, helped 200 students excel in their studies through extra classes, and nurtured over 100 young athletes.
