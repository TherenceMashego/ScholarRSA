Our Programs

At Scholar SA, we offer comprehensive programs designed to support children's education, personal development, and athletic potential. Each program addresses specific needs to help children thrive.

School Uniforms & Shoes

This program provides school uniforms and shoes to children in need to ensure they can attend school with confidence and dignity.

500+ children supported

Support This Program

Extra Classes

We offer extra classes to help children who need additional support with their studies, ensuring they don't fall behind academically.

200+ students helped

Support This Program

Sports Talent Support

This program aims to identify and support children with sports talent, providing them with the resources to excel in their chosen sport.

100+ athletes nurtured

Support This Program

Feminine Products Support

We provide feminine hygiene products to young girls to ensure they can attend school without missing days due to lack of access to these necessities.

300+ girls supported

Program Impact

Academic Improvement

85% of students in our extra classes program show measurable improvement in their grades within 3 months.

Increased Confidence

92% of uniform recipients report feeling more confident attending school.

School Attendance

Feminine product distribution has reduced absenteeism among girls by 75%.
