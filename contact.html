Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with Scholar SA?

We're always looking for passionate volunteers! Please fill out the contact form above or email us directly to learn about current volunteer opportunities.

How are donations used?

90% of all donations go directly to program expenses (uniforms, school supplies, sports equipment). The remaining 10% supports operational costs.

Can I donate items instead of money?

Yes! We accept new school uniforms, shoes, and feminine hygiene products. Please contact us to arrange a donation drop-off.
